## Vision

To make life easier and more purpose-driven by bringing verified knowledge of what it means to live well to every person on earth. All information and methods — including the full depth of human wisdom across cultures and generations — available and obtainable for everyone to benefit from.

**Not handing out salmon. Teaching people to fish.**

---

## What ATLAS Is

ATLAS is a seven-layer AI research platform where every piece of knowledge is verified, epistemically tagged, and governed entirely by the community that contributes to it.

- Every finding traceable to its source, author, and confidence level
- Expertise weighted by demonstrated credential, not institutional affiliation
- Governance by community vote, recorded on a blockchain that cannot be altered
- A Non-Fiction LLM trained exclusively on verified, community-governed knowledge
- Three constitutional laws no vote, credential, or authority can override
- Designed to detect corruption faster than corruption can entrench itself

This is not a search engine. Not a chatbot. It is the knowledge infrastructure layer that has always been missing beneath the internet.

---

## The Three Laws

The constitutional foundation of the platform. No governance decision, no majority vote, no administrative authority can override them.

1. **Individual Wellbeing** — the platform protects the person first
2. **Community Circulation** — knowledge must move and compound, not pool
3. **Ecological Regeneration** — all outputs are measured against the living world

## Joining the Founding Team

We are looking for a small founding cohort — not a large team. People who want to own a piece of the architecture, not execute someone else's roadmap.

**Open roles:**
- Blockchain Architect
- AI / ML Engineer
- Backend Engineer
- Frontend Engineer
- Security Engineer

**What founding team membership means:**

Founding team members receive a permanent **Founding Accolade** — a blockchain-anchored designation embedded in the platform from genesis, irremovable and historically permanent. They carry standing invitation to become the **Expansion Team** as the platform scales. And they hold foundational stake in a contribution economy that grows more valuable as it demonstrates that purpose-driven work generates more real value than financial accumulation.

There is no salary and no external funding. This project is built on contribution, expertise, and shared stake in what it becomes.

**You are the right person if:**
- You think in systems, not just components
- You want your work to matter beyond the product cycle
- You can hold philosophical and technical concerns simultaneously
- You are comfortable with a long build — this is a foundation, not a sprint
